About me

I'm a Mechatronics engineer holding a M.Sc. in Mechatronics Engineering from the American University of Sharjah (AUS), and a B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering (sub-branch Mechatronics) from the Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST). I live in UAE, Al-Ain, working as a research assistant in the United Arab Emirates University (UAEU). I'm Currently working on two projects; 1) building a small-scale concrete 3D printer, and 2) designing a wearable hand assistive device for stroke patients.
Prior to my current appointment and during my Mater's study at AUS, I was a teaching and research assistant. My research load was focused on my thesis topic which is about a map exploration strategy for multiple robots (more details in the "projects" section). Before I joined AUS, I was in Jordan (where I originally come from), I was working as a junior Automation Engineer at Al-Wefaq Control Systems.
